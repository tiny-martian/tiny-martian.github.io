I love that I live in a world where I can buy woodcut posters online—so authentic yet so modern!—and they’ll arrive within the week. Imagine that, less than a week between hot fresh ink seeping into a piece of paper in Portland and a cool, smooth poster all rolled up in a cardboard tube dropping onto my doorstep in Boulder! Like magic. Email, clothing, pizza appears out of thin air. All I have to do is click a few buttons, and centuries compress.
Near instant gratification, and it just keeps getting more and more instant.
